Congratulations. You’re about to enjoy an intriguing murder mystery. Get ready for tons of fun as you slip into a hot bath of mystery, intrigue, and mullet clippings. Let’s hope you catch the future killer that is among you.

There are three rounds of the game.

Round One:
You’ll watch a video from Anabelle Freebyrd, a town gossip, to get up to speed. Then you’ll participate in a group challenge to identify a notorious porch pirate. Clue cards will be distributed. Some clues are shared, others are secret.

Round Two:
After solving the porch pirate case, new cards will be distributed. A murder will occur. You must interrogate, investigate, and share all ROUND TWO clues.

Round Three:
Players accuse the murderer. The solution is revealed and confessions unfold in order.
